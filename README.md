Title: Exploring-Protocols - Monard

Author: Aidan Monard

Summary 

This tutorial introduces beginners to the concept of digital protocols and standards that allow computers, networks, and devices to communicate. It explains what protocols are, why they matter, and how popular protocols such as HTTP, HTTPS, FTP, TCP/IP, and DNS function. The tutorial provides understandable examples and diagrams to help readers build a understanding of how data travels across digital systems.

Target Audience

This tutorial is created for beginners, especially high school or early college students, who have little to no background in networking or computer science. No prior knowledge is required, just the want to learn more.
